COMPANY OVERVIEW

RADAR Consultancy Inc is an established consulting firm led by results-oriented professionals and hospitality management practitioners. It’s service includes Franchising Support, Training, Business Development, Recruitment and Marketing. It has also built partnership with several hotel and restaurant brands and experts to deliver client responsive solutions throughout Asia Pacific and beyond.

BUSINESS PHILOSOPHY

  • Nurturing young up and coming talent within our industry whether it be in service, management or in the culinary arts.
  • To always be at the forefront of the ever changing and highly competitive food industry.
  • Quality at low cost; to provide our clients with the very best service in the industry from planning and marketing to management and maintenance at prices that won't break the bank.
  • Protecting the environment. We hope to set an example to other businesses in all industries by showing them how they can still achieve great profits while not damaging the environment.
